The World on a Screen

We spend our lives in screens. All our messaging is on screens. It's quick. It's engaging. And gone in a flash. No moment to treasure. Nothing to hold on to. Nothing to stick on the fridge door.

Our Simple Mission: Deliver Joy

SmileMail exists simply to bring some joy into people’s homes. To send or receive a physical postcard from a friend will bring some joy. Not in seconds, but in days. Instant gratification replaced by long-lasting memorable moments.

Our "Always Free" Promise

It's free to use. Totally free. For as long as we can. We’ll print and post your postcards, usually the next working day.

SmileMail is funded by partners who choose to promote physical human interaction in this otherwise digital world. You are free to use the app, and please share it with your friends. Our aim, our hope, is that thousands of postcards are sent every day, bringing a little joy into people’s homes.

Your Trust is Everything

Your data is safe. We don’t and never will sell it. Not even to our funding partners. Recipient data is deleted after 30 days. To help keep this service free, some postcards may carry a small advertisement from one of our partners.
